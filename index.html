FAQs

What buyers ask first

Can we start without a sales call?

Yes. Use Gate intake or Trust Brief intake with a Request ID — public forms stay non-confidential. Procurement and bank lanes support engagement-artifact onboarding when policy requires.

What do we buy first?

Most teams start with Copilot QuickScan or Readiness Pack for rollout evidence. Trust Brief is the six-week diagnostic land SKU before enterprise scale. Bank pilot is a separate shadow-evaluate SOW.

How does this differ from technical AI due diligence?

Technical DD covers models, code, and moat. Noetfield covers governance and human-architecture — exportable go/no-go trails, approver chains, and board exports. See investor diligence lane for buy-side packaging.

Who holds data and settles funds?

Noetfield is non-custodial — no payment initiation, settlement, or fund movement. M365 evidence is metadata-only by default. Your institution remains accountable for production decisions.
