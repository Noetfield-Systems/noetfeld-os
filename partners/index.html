Partner programs · Canada 2026–27

AI governance and risk intelligence for licensed execution partners

Noetfield is a pre-execution control layer for banks, credit unions, licensed Canadian VASPs and exchanges, and RPAA-supervised PSPs. We evaluate operational intent, record Trust Ledger evidence, and return allow or deny — your systems execute; we never hold custody or move funds.

What partners get

  • Evaluate API — POST /api/v1/governance/evaluate with shadow mode for pilots
  • Trust Ledger — immutable audit export keyed by Request ID (RID)
  • Read-only signals — operational webhooks into governance (no order placement from Noetfield)
  • Webhooks — governance.decision.recorded for SIEM / GRC tooling

Canada regulatory alignment (adjacency only)

We help partners produce evidence for OSFI E-23 (third-party AI), Consumer-Driven Banking policy gates, and stablecoin/PSP supervision timelines — as a software vendor, not an issuer, MSB, or RPAA registrant.

Integration pattern

Your orchestrator calls evaluate with a RID → on allow, your licensed API executes → optional read-only status webhooks → audit export for vendor DD.
