Phase 3 — Product discovery

Every engagement feeds a workflow intelligence database — the factory for SaaS ideas.

Opportunity scoring framework

score = (time_saved × 0.3) + (revenue_impact × 0.3) + (frequency × 0.15) + (vertical_fit × 0.15) − (complexity × 0.1) — promote to SaaS when score ≥ 70 across 3+ deployments.
