The gap

Finance sees invoices. Engineering sees tokens. Governance sees neither together.

Enterprise AI teams inherit fragmented signals: cloud billing exports, model-provider dashboards, agent logs, and policy documents that drift from production. AI Value Governance OS unifies cost, usage, value, risk, and execution authority on one institutional control-plane infrastructure.

02

Attribution & KPIs

Workflow attribution and business KPI mapping

Every inference path should answer two questions: which workflow consumed it, and what business outcome it supports. AI Value Governance OS attributes spend and usage to named workflows, teams, and approved use cases — then maps those signals to the KPIs your finance and product leaders already track.

Attribution

Workflow attribution

Tag inference and agent runs by workflow, team, environment, and model tier — so cost conversations start with named owners, not aggregate invoices.

KPIs

Business KPI mapping

Link approved use cases to outcome metrics — throughput, resolution time, error rate, revenue impact — so governance decisions connect to business value, not token counts alone.

Telemetry

Execution telemetry

Capture evaluate events, model routing, latency, and decision outcomes as first-party telemetry — the spine for dashboards, audit, and optimization.

Dashboards

Governance dashboards

Roll up spend, usage, policy outcomes, and KPI trends in views built for finance, risk, and engineering — one control plane, role-appropriate surfaces.
