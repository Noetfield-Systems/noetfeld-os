## Categories

### Essential (always on)

Required for the site to remember your cookie choice and maintain basic session reliability. These cannot be disabled while using the site.

| Name | Storage | Duration | Purpose |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| `noos_cookie_consent_v1` | localStorage | Until cleared | Stores your consent decision |

### Analytics (optional)

If you opt in, we may enable privacy-respecting usage measurement on this domain in the future. **No third-party analytics scripts are loaded today.**

## Managing preferences

Open **Cookie preferences** from the footer banner at any time. Your choice is stored locally in your browser.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, use the Partner Gateway intake form or your existing Noetfield operations contact.
