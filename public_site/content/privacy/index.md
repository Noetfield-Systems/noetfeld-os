## Scope

This notice applies to the Noetfield Systems institutional site (Trust Ledger and Partner Gateway). It does not replace product-specific privacy terms for Noetfield, SourceA, or TrustField services hosted elsewhere.

## Information we process

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Partner intake form fields | Review integration inquiries | Operational records per intake workflow |
| Essential cookies / local storage | Remember cookie preferences | Until you clear browser storage |
| Server access logs | Security and reliability | Short operational retention |

We do not sell personal information. Analytics cookies are **off by default** until you opt in.

## Your choices

Use **Cookie preferences** in the site footer to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You may request access or deletion of intake data by contacting operations through your submitted corporate domain channel.

## Updates

We may update this notice when site functionality changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated version marker in the page header.
