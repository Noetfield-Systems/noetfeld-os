How It Works

Noetfield standardizes payment intent and hands off execution to licensed downstream partners.

01 — Source

SME platform sends payout amount, currency, and beneficiary metadata.

02 — Noetfield Layer

We validate parameters, normalize data, map compliance tags, and create immutable instruction state.

03 — Regulated Execution

Licensed partners such as NDAX process FX and settlement under their regulatory permissions.

04 — End State

Webhook updates synchronize delivery status back to the SME platform.
