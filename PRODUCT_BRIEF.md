# Product brief (external)

## Definition

Noetfield is governance execution infrastructure that evaluates AI-driven operational intent before execution in regulated environments.

## Value

Regulated organizations adopt copilots and automation faster than policy can keep pace. Noetfield provides a governance evaluation layer that checks whether a proposed action is allowed under your rules, records a defensible compliance log, and returns a clear allow or deny decision before any external system acts. The product does not move money, hold assets, or execute transactions—it reduces regulatory and operational exposure by making governance decisions visible, consistent, and reviewable for boards, risk officers, and technology leaders.
