## Six-week deliverables (W1–W6)

| Week | Milestone | Deliverable | Acceptance |
|------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **W1** | Kickoff + scope | Engagement charter · RID assigned · stakeholder map | Named contacts · scope boundaries signed |
| **W2** | Materiality | AI use-case inventory · materiality matrix | Top 10 use cases ranked by governance impact |
| **W3** | Policy map | AI policy gap analysis vs stated controls | Gap table with owner + remediation priority |
| **W4** | Risk exposure | Risk exposure analysis (orientation) | Executive risk summary draft |
| **W5** | Controls + TLE | Starter Trust Ledger format · sample evaluate orientation | One orientation TLE or evaluate walkthrough |
| **W6** | Board handoff | **Board memo** · executive summary · handoff pack | Board-ready PDF delivered · kickoff call for Copilot pilot upgrade path |
