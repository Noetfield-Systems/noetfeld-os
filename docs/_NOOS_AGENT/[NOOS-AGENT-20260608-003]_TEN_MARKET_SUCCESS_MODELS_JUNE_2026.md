## What the market is proving (June 2026 — patterns, not war)

### 1. Pre-execution is a real category now

Exogram, WhiteFin, Execlave, Cordum-style guides, and Fiddler’s “control plane” language all converge on: **evaluate before dispatch**, fail-closed, immutable audit. This validates Noetfield’s parent-brand thesis on [noetfield.com](https://noetfield.com).

### 2. Two monetization highways

| Highway | Examples | Fit for Noetfield OS |
|---------|----------|----------------------|
| **Enterprise GRC** ($50K–$500K/yr) | Credo, Holistic, IBM, OneTrust | GEL + Trust Ledger SKU; bank pilot |
| **Developer gate** (freemium → team → enterprise) | Execlave, Exogram-style | `api.noetfield.com` + sandbox after auth |

### 3. Regulated buyers pay for **proof**, not dashboards

Fiddler FS case study: deal unlocked by **in-VPC enforcement** (no data leaving perimeter). Holistic/Credo: **audit-ready artifacts**. Our equivalent: append-only decision log + replay + drift export.

### 4. M&A validates mid-market builds

FairNow → AuditBoard shows a **$30K–$100K/yr** governance product can become strategic to a GRC platform. Long-term optionality for Noetfield OS — not near-term goal.

### 5. Agent governance ≠ decision governance (but overlap)

WhiteFin/Execlave = **tool-call** governance. Noetfield OS = **structured operational decision** governance (credit, eligibility, policy corridors). Adjacent markets; learn enforcement patterns, don’t copy scope.

## Regulatory tailwind (context for all ten)

**SR 26-2** (US agencies, April 17, 2026) refreshed model risk management guidance — emphasizes risk-based governance and audit of MRM **process**, while noting **future** guidance for GenAI/agentic systems. Buyers are preparing **parallel** AI governance stacks now ([Federal Reserve SR 26-2](https://www.federalreserve.gov/supervisionreg/srletters/SR2602.pdf)).

Canadian + EU AI Act timelines (2026–2027) keep **evidence** budgets open — aligns with Credo/Holistic/FairNow success, not with generic dev tools.

To stay specialized (per `NOOS-AGENT-20260529-002`):

- Model training / LLM hosting
- General agent tool proxy for all SaaS APIs (WhiteFin class)
- ML observability-only without policy decision log (WhyLabs/Arize class)

**Our wedge:** narrow **Governance Execution Layer** for **regulated operational decisions** — Canadian first, non-custodial, audit + replay + drift.
