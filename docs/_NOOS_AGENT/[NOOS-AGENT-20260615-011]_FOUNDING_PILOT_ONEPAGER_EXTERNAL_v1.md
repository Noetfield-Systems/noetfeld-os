## What Noetfield does

Noetfield governs **before execution**. Every material action is gated by policy at dispatch, written to a signed receipt ledger, and protected by tamper detection.

| **Board PDF** | Governance meeting pack |

| **Shadow mode** | Runs **parallel** to your stack — no production cutover |
