# RBC FinSec Incubator — application narrative (draft)

**Disclaimer:** Noetfield is not affiliated with or endorsed by Royal Bank of Canada unless a separate agreement exists.

## One-liner

Security-first **pre-execution governance API** for fintech founders building API-first products — evaluate intent, Trust Ledger audit export, shadow mode for pilots.

## Problem

Fintechs integrate AI and trading APIs before policy and evidence keep pace. Incubator cohorts need **defensible controls** without becoming MSBs or RPAA registrants themselves.

## Solution

Noetfield control layer (`POST /api/v1/governance/evaluate`) + Trust Ledger + optional read-only partner signals. Licensed partners execute; Noetfield never holds custody.

## Fit for FinSec

- Aligns with security-first cohort curriculum (policy before production APIs)
- RID lineage for demo day diligence conversations
- Canada regulatory adjacency: E-23, CDB, stablecoin supervision timelines

## Ask

Office hours or design-partner slot for 1–2 cohort companies in **shadow** integration.
