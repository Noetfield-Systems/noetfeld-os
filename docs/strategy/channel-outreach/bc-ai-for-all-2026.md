### What Noetfield is

**Governance execution infrastructure** for regulated organizations adopting AI — especially **Microsoft 365 Copilot**. Noetfield evaluates operational intent against policy, records immutable audit evidence (Trust Ledger / TLE), and returns **allow / reject / review** **before** partner or production systems execute.

### What Noetfield is not

- Not a payment service provider, custodian, or money transmitter.
- Not sovereign cloud / compute infrastructure or a public AI supercomputer vendor.
- Not a consumer parental-controls or creative-generative app.
- Not a substitute for federal or provincial regulation — we produce **evidence** buyers need under emerging rules.
