# POSA SaaS - First 100 Users Launch Strategy v1.0

Document key: `posa-saas-first-100-users-launch-strategy-v1`

Status in source: go-to-market execution plan

## Normalized purpose

This document defines a zero-to-100-paying-users launch strategy for POSA SaaS,
focused on validating whether people will pay for an AI system that helps them
generate revenue automatically.

## Key content

- Ideal early adopters: agency owners, freelancers, consultants, coaches, solo
  SaaS founders, lead-generation operators
- Entry offer: done-for-you POSA setup
- Pricing: early validation price and optional pro setup
- Acquisition channels: direct outbound, Upwork, content, referrals
- Delivery strategy: install lead scraping, outreach automation, tracking
  sheet, and message templates in 24-72 hours
- Metrics: messages sent, response rate, conversions, users, revenue
