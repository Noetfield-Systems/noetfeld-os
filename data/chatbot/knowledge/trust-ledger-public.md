# Trust Ledger vs Trust Brief

## Trust Ledger (product infrastructure)

The **Trust Ledger** is Noetfield's immutable governance compliance log:

- Records decisions, policy references, and Request ID (RID) lineage
- Produces **Trust Ledger Entries (TLEs)** — tamper-evident receipts
- Exportable for vendor due diligence, procurement, and board packs
- Public orientation: `/trust-ledger/` on www

Positioning: *We produce the audit trail your Copilot deployment will be asked for later.*

## Trust Brief (consulting SKU — $10,000)

Six-week **engagement**: governance audit, AI policy mapping, risk exposure analysis. Land diagnostic before enterprise scale.

Intake: `/trust-brief/intake/`

## Key difference

| | Trust Ledger | Trust Brief |
|---|--------------|-------------|
| **Type** | Product / audit trail infrastructure | Paid consulting engagement |
| **Price** | Part of runtime tiers (see `/runtime/`) | **$10,000** fixed engagement |
| **Outcome** | Ongoing signed decision records | Six-week audit deliverable |

Both support Copilot governance motion; they are **not** the same SKU.
