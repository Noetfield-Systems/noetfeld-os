# Noetfield pricing matrix (distilled from www)

## Summary
Noetfield Intelligence for Canadian SMEs (Diagnostic from $2,500) · Copilot Governance Pack for regulated institutions ($2k–10k) · same governed spine underneath.

## Noetfield Intelligence (SME)

### Diagnostic Sprint
**Price:** $2,500–$5,000
1-week ops audit · top 3 automations · roadmap PDF · no code

### Automation Build
**Price:** $8,000+
2–3 production workflows · handoff · replay demo

### AI Ops Partner
**Price:** $3,000/mo
Monitor · tune · deploy · strategic guidance

### Vertical SaaS
**Price:** $99–499/mo
ImmigrationOS · DiasporaBiz · BrokerBrain — after 3+ same-vertical clients

## Access paths and contract programs

### Developer access · free
**Price:** $0
Self-serve sandbox — evaluate API, workspace, mock M365 connectors. 14-day sandbox · 50 evaluate calls · mock M365 connectors · no sales call.

### Lead program · apply online
**Price:** $2k–10k
Copilot Governance Pack — production tenant, board PDF in governance meeting, procurement ZIP.

### Contract SKU
**Price:** $10,000
Trust Brief — six-week governance diagnostic before Copilot or automation scale.

### Contract SKU · custom
**Price:** Enterprise
Bank Pilot shadow simulation — read-only governance evaluate for FRFI and regulated institutions.

## Sandbox vs production

| Capability | Sandbox | Production |
|---|---|---|
| Evaluate API | 50 calls / 14 days | Per SOW · tenant-scoped keys |
| M365 connectors | Mock OAuth | Metadata-only Purview · Entra · audit |
| TLE export | Sample YAML + orientation PDF | Board PDF + procurement ZIP |
| Policy automation | Investigate · triage · draft (sandbox) | Full chain + named approvers |
| Sales call required | No | Program intake · SOW |
| Signed go/no-go per Copilot decision | TLE v1 · confidence score · Request ID lineage Board and risk committee evidence | Board PDF used in governance meeting — pilot success signal |
| Procurement and legal diligence | Procurement ZIP · fail-closed export integrity verification M365 metadata evidence index | Purview · Entra · audit connectors — read-only · metadata-only |

## Milestone ladder

- **$2k–10k** — Copilot Governance Pack · 90 days · board PDF
- **$10k** — Trust Brief · 6 weeks · policy map + risk exposure
- **Custom** — Bank Pilot · enterprise SOW · shadow → enforce

## Contract SKUs (locked)

Three contract SKUs only: Copilot Governance Pack ($2k–10k), Trust Brief ($10k), Bank Pilot (custom).
Free sandbox is developer access — not a fourth retail SKU.
