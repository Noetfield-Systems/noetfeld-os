How it works

Three steps, full visibility.

From initiation to settlement, every step is logged, reconciled, and auditable.

01
🔍
You initiate

Enter your payment details. Noetfield's routing engine selects the optimal execution path across bank wire, stablecoin, and MSB rails — showing you the exact fee before you confirm.

02
⚡
We route & execute

TrustField Settlement executes via the selected rail. Your funds move through a segregated FBO account — fully separated from operating funds — with every state transition logged.

03
📋
You receive proof

Upon settlement you receive a complete Trust Brief: reconciliation report, FINTRAC-ready documentation, and a regulator-readable audit package. Your compliance team stays clean.
