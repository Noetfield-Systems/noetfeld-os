Regulatory Infrastructure Architecture

Noetfield Technologies Inc. is a financial infrastructure technology provider offering non-custodial data mediation routing services. Noetfield does not function as a bank, licensed money services business (MSB), custodial wallet operator, or cryptocurrency exchange asset manager.

All payment conversions, fiat-to-digital currency clearing, asset settlement balances, and institutional vaulting pipelines are processed exclusively through registered downstream regulated financial service partners, including NDAX.
